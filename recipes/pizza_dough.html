2 Ingredient Pizza Dough

Simple Two Ingredient Homemade Pizza Dough

Description

Having Friday night pizza at home just got easier, and cheaper! Only needs 2 ingredients and little bit of time.

Ingredients

  • Self Rising Flour
  • Greek Yogurt
Steps:
  1. Mix the ingredents together
  2. Knead for 8-10 minutes
  3. Parbake at 500 degrees for 5 min
  4. Add the toppings.
  5. Finish baking, pulling out when the crust is golden brown.
