Worlds Best Lasagna

Description

The worlds best lasagna is a labor of love. She consists of 14 hours of work, talent and lots of patience. The recipe uses simple ingredents, but utilizes the best version of those we could source. We didn't use just any ricotta. We bought fresh cream from a farm, milked this morning, and make our own riccota. We think you should too! Voted #1 lasagna, 6 years running by the 2rd grade Boys and Girls Club.

Ingredients

  • Meat Sauce
  • Noodles
  • Ricotta Mix
  • Mozzarella, Sliced
  • Parmesan Cheese
Steps:
  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the Ricotta Mix
  4. Layer the lasagna
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let rest 30 miutes before serving.
