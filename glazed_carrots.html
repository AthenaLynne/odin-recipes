Brown Sugar Glazed Carrots

Glazed Carrots

Description

Can't Get Your Kids to Eat Their Veggies? Saute in brown sugar and they won't leave a single carrot behind.

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds carrots, peeled and chopped
  • 1/4 C Brown Sugar
  • 1/4 C Butter
  • Pinch of Salt, to taste
  • Pinch Pepper, to taste
Steps:
  1. Mix the ingredents together
  2. Add to saucepan
  3. Cook on med-low heat until carrots are desired softness
  4. Drizzle with honey for extra sweetness if desired.
